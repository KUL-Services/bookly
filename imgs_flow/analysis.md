# Staff Management Flow Analysis

## Current Implementation Status

Based on the existing codebase, here's what's currently implemented:

### ✅ Currently Implemented:

1. **Staff Shifts Timeline View**

   - Daily view with time slots (10:00 AM - 12:00 AM)
   - Staff list with working hours display
   - Business hours indicator
   - Closed day handling (recently added)

2. **Staff Data Structure**
   - Staff profiles with names
   - Working hours per day
   - Shift durations and breaks

### ❌ Missing Features (Common in Booking Systems):

1. **Staff Profile Management**

   - Add/Edit/Delete staff members
   - Staff contact information
   - Staff roles and permissions
   - Staff availability settings

2. **Service Assignment**

   - Assign services to staff members
   - Service duration and pricing per staff
   - Staff specializations

3. **Advanced Scheduling Features**

   - Drag & drop shift editing
   - Bulk schedule updates
   - Recurring schedule templates
   - Schedule conflicts detection

4. **Time Off Management**

   - Request time off
   - Approve/deny time off requests
   - Holiday management
   - Sick leave tracking

5. **Performance Analytics**
   - Staff utilization rates
   - Revenue per staff member
   - Customer ratings per staff
   - Booking completion rates
